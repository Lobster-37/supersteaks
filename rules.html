The Simple Rules: How It Works

SuperSteaks is all about free fun and luck! Here's the 3-step process to get involved in our random team assignment draws. Tournament winners receive £5.

1

🎉 Enter a Free Draw

Check the Our Contests page and enter a competition draw—football tournaments, cricket leagues, rugby cups, and more! All draws are always 100% free to enter.

2

🎲 Get Your Random Team

Our system will randomly assign you one of the teams in the competition. You can't choose or change it—that's what makes it exciting! It's pure luck of the draw.

3

🏆 Cheer & Win Prizes!

Now you cheer for your randomly assigned team! If they win the actual competition, you receive £5 as the tournament winner.
