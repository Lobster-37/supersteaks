How to Install

üì± Mobile Installation

iPhone/iPad

1. Open SuperSteaks in Safari
2. Tap the Share button
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add" to confirm

Android Phone

1. Open SuperSteaks in Chrome or Edge
2. Tap the menu (three dots) in top right
3. Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Install" to confirm

üíª Desktop Installation

Chrome/Edge

1. Open SuperSteaks in your browser
2. Look for the install icon in the address bar (usually on the right)
3. Click the icon and select "Install"
4. Choose where to save the app shortcut

Other Browsers

1. Open SuperSteaks in your browser
2. Right-click and select "Create shortcut" or "Create app shortcut"
3. Choose to save to desktop or start menu
