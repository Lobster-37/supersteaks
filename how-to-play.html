How to Play SuperSteaks

1

Create Your Account

Start by creating a free SuperSteaks account using your email address. This is your gateway to the exciting world of competitive sports contests.

  • Provide a valid email address
  • Choose a unique username
  • Create a secure password
  • Verify your email to activate your account
2

Browse Tournaments

Visit our Tournaments page to see all available competitions. We feature major football tournaments throughout the year including international competitions and top league tournaments.

  • View tournament name and description
  • Check player count and status
  • See the scoring system
  • Review the tournament schedule
3

Join a Tournament

Select a tournament that interests you and click "Join" to enter the competition. You'll be automatically assigned a team from the tournament's roster.

  • Click the "Join Tournament" button
  • System randomly assigns you a team
  • You'll be notified of your team assignment
  • Enter the tournament lobby
4

Enter the Lobby

After joining, you'll enter a lobby where you can see other players and real-time tournament information. The lobby is your hub for tournament interaction.

  • View your assigned team
  • See other players in the tournament
  • Check the tournament status
  • Monitor live tournament updates
5

Follow Your Team

Once assigned a team, you'll follow their performance throughout the tournament. Your team's results will determine your score and ranking, and tournament winners receive ¬£5.

  • Track match results and scores
  • Monitor your team's progression
  • See how you rank against other players
  • Earn points based on team performance
  • Win ¬£5 if your team wins the tournament
6

Compete & Win

Compete against other players throughout the tournament and aim for the top of the leaderboard. The player whose team performs best wins the tournament!

  • Earn points from team victories
  • Climb the leaderboard
  • Compete for tournament bragging rights
  • Join multiple tournaments simultaneously

Scoring System

Win

+3 points - Your team wins their match

Draw

+1 point - Your team draws their match

Loss

0 points - Your team loses their match

Advancement

+5 bonus points - Your team advances in tournament
