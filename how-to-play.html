How to Play SuperSteaks

1

Create Your Account

Sign up with your email, choose a username, and you are ready to enter tournaments.

2

Browse Tournaments

Go to Tournaments, pick one that is open, and check how many spots are left.

3

Join and Get a Team

When you join, your team is assigned at random for fair play.

4

Use the Lobby

In your lobby you can see your team, upcoming matches, and other players in the same tournament.

5

Follow Results

Track fixtures and results as matches are played and watch the leaderboard update.

App Version

SuperSteaks can be installed as an app-style experience on both mobile and desktop.

Mobile: Open SuperSteaks in your browser and choose Add to Home Screen or Install app.

Desktop: In Chrome or Edge, use the install icon in the address bar (or browser menu) to install SuperSteaks as an app window.
